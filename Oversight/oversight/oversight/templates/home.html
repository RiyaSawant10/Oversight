Oversight

Oversight is a dynamic web platform that offers a myriad of useful features for students, researchers, and professionals alike. With its advanced note-taking functionality, users can capture and organize their thoughts and ideas with ease. The state-of-the-art chatbot provides quick and accurate responses to queries, making communication effortless and streamlined. Additionally, the cutting-edge project ideas generation feature assists users in generating innovative and creative ideas for their next big project. Moreover, the student admission prediction tool uses sophisticated algorithms and machine learning techniques to predict a user's chances of getting admitted to their desired program or institution. Overall, Oversight is a comprehensive and intuitive web app that empowers its users to achieve their academic goals
